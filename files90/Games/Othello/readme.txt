This is a classic Othello clone game, that me and my Students have
developed in the classroom for educational reasons. I have designed the
bitmaps and provided them to the class on a disk. Then we designed and
programmed the whole project in two lessons.



 Installation
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Please unzip the contents of OTHELLOxxx.ZIP file to a folder of your choice.


 Execution
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Run othello.exe


 Un-Install
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Delete the folder you installed Othello in. Othello WILL NOT write anything 
 to your computer's registry, or any other folders of your disk. 
 Installation and Uninstallation is easy!


 Help
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

 Play against the computer or a friend. You can choose between three AI
 levels for the computer player (Beginner, Intermediate, Pro) and you can
 also simulate a game by putting a computer player against another 
 computer player.

 Right-click on the left hand side of the main window to bring up the menu.

 Using the menu you can:
 - change the animation speed
 - choose a different skin
 - change the computer's AI level
 - display the about box
